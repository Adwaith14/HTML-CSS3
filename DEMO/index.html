Grandma's Famous Lasagna

By Maria Rossi. Published on July 15, 2025

This is not just any lasagna. This is a recipe passed down through three generations, perfected over time to become the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 large white onion, minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ¬æ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Season with sugar, fennel seeds, basil, and salt. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving.

Essential Kitchen Tools

  • 9x13-inch Baking Dish

    For perfect layers.

  • Large Dutch Oven

    To simmer the sauce.

  • Large Pot

    For boiling noodles.
